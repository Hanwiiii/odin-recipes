Egg Drop Soup with Chicken

Description

Comforting savory bowl of egg drop soup with delicious chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 tsp soy sauce
  • Knob of ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp seasame oil
