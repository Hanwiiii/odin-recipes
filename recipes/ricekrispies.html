Rice Krispie Treats

Description

Deliciously sweet rice krispie treats

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4 cups mini marshmallow
  • 5 cups crispy rice cereal

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat
  2. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 mins longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.
  4. Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13 inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper.
  5. Cut into desired shapes when cooled.
